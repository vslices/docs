# Human Factors

Human Factors explores patterns that emerge within individuals throughout their professional lives.

Unlike technical challenges, these patterns are often difficult to recognize while they are happening. Many appear as:

- frustration
- burnout
- self-doubt
- overcommitment
- responsibility
- ambition
- growth
- profesional identity
- personal identity

Human Factors does not attempt to provide psychological guidance, therapy or coaching; it focuses on observations gathered from real software projects and professional experience.

The purpose is not to prescribe behavior, but to help engineers recognize situations they may eventually encounter themselves.
