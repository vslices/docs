# Collaboration Model

VSlices Method can distribute the design iteration flow across different people or teams.

The stages are responsibilities, not mandatory roles. 

- A small team may perform all stages together.
- A larger organization may split the stages across specialized teams.

The important rule is continuity: each team should preserve the intent discovered by the previous stage and return new learning to the shared context.

In the archaeology metaphor, every team participates in excavation. But each team may work at a different depth:

- one team may explore broadly to discover domain signals,
- another may organize and catalog what was discovered,
- another may interpret the findings and decide where value may exist,
- another may build from those findings and return feedback.

The number of teams is not fixed.

VSlices Method can work with one team, two teams, three teams, or four teams.

The structure should adapt to the organization, the organization should not be forced to adapt to the method.
