# Alive Lab glossary

This glossary defines terms used by Alive Lab.

Alive Lab is the space where VSlices ideas, patterns, documents, methods, and abstractions are tested against real work before being treated as stable product concepts.

These terms help preserve the relationship between observation, validation, learning, and product evolution.

## Validation concepts

- __Alive Lab__: a real project, scenario, or working environment used to validate VSlices ideas, patterns, documents, methods, and abstractions before treating them as stable product concepts.

- __Validation__: evidence gathered to confirm, reject, or refine an assumption, decision, behavior, document, pattern, method, implementation, or product idea.

- __Evidence__: observable information produced by real work, usage, feedback, implementation, documentation, failure, or review.

- __Learning__: knowledge produced after observing, validating, implementing, reviewing, or evolving something in a real context.

- __Experiment__: a limited validation attempt used to test whether an idea, document, abstraction, pattern, method, or implementation direction is useful in practice. In Alive Lab, an experiment is not a required documentation section.

## Knowledge artifacts

- __Field Note__: a lightweight learning artifact used to preserve observations from real work before they become stronger guidance.

- __Project Story__: a narrative artifact used to preserve how real work evolved, what was learned, and what evidence shaped the direction.

- __Observation__: something noticed during real work that may reveal a pattern, risk, learning opportunity, design tension, documentation need, or product improvement.

## Product evolution concepts

- __Product candidate__: an idea, pattern, document, abstraction, or method that may become part of VSlices after enough validation.

- __Stable concept__: a VSlices concept that has enough practical evidence to be documented as part of a product, method, pattern, or guidance.

- __Product guidance__: guidance extracted from validated learning to help people use, interpret, or evolve one part of the VSlices suite.

- __Validated learning__: learning supported by practical evidence from real projects, real usage, implementation feedback, documentation work, or repeated observation.

## Relationship between the terms

Alive Lab connects real work with VSlices product evolution.

```text
Observation
-> Experiment
-> Validation
-> Learning
-> Product guidance
-> Stable concept
```

This is not a rigid process. A concept may move slowly, return to observation, or remain experimental for a long time.

The important part is that VSlices should not treat ideas as stable only because they are theoretically interesting. Concepts should become stronger when real work provides evidence that they are useful.
