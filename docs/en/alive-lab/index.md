# Alive Lab

Alive Lab is the experimental space where VSlices ideas are observed, tested, questioned, and refined before becoming stable product guidance. It exists because VSlices should not evolve only from abstract design.

The suite should grow from real or simulated project work, concrete problems, repeated patterns, validation, and lessons learned over time.

## Purpose

Alive Lab helps preserve learning while it is still evolving. Its purpose is to capture:

* project experience
* design experiments
* documentation experiments
* architectural observations
* framework exploration
* early theories
* validation notes
* lessons that may later influence VSlices Method, Design, Docs Standard, or Framework

Not everything in Alive Lab is final. 

Some ideas may become official guidance. Some may remain contextual. Some may be changed, reduced, postponed, or rejected. That is intentional.

## Why Alive Lab exists

VSlices is built around progressive complexity and structural continuity.

Because of that, new ideas should not become official parts of the suite too early. Alive Lab provides a place to preserve exploration without pretending that every idea is already stable.

It helps separate:

* what has been observed
* what is being tested
* what seems promising
* what needs more evidence
* what should become product guidance later

The goal is to learn before standardizing.

## How to read this section

Alive Lab content should be read as exploratory material.

It may include incomplete reasoning, early project observations, changing conclusions, or ideas that are still being validated.

This section is not a source of official rules. Official guidance belongs in the product sections:

* VSlices Method
* VSlices Design
* VSlices Docs Standard
* VSlices Framework

Alive Lab explains how some of that guidance may emerge.

## Relationship with VSlices products

Alive Lab may influence any VSlices product.

- A project story may reveal a documentation need.
- A theory may suggest a design technique.
- An experiment may expose a framework primitive.
- A repeated observation may eventually become part of VSlices Method.

However, Alive Lab does not automatically define product direction.

Ideas should move from Alive Lab into official VSlices guidance only when they have enough evidence, usefulness, and coherence with the suite philosophy.

## Current state

Alive Lab is still early.

For now, it mainly exists to preserve the space where future project learning, theories, and experiments can live. More content will be added as VSlices is tested through concrete work.
