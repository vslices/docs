# Theories

Theories are exploratory hypotheses used to investigate ideas that may influence VSlices in the future.

They are not official product direction.

They are not validated methodology.

They exist to preserve early reasoning before it is either:

* validated,
* refined,
* rejected,
* or transformed into product documentation.

## Purpose

Theories help us explore:

* recurring patterns
* conceptual models
* possible abstractions
* relationships between ideas
* unresolved engineering questions

## Important Note

A theory should not become part of VSlices only because it is interesting.

A theory may influence VSlices only after it helps explain or solve real problems observed in real projects.
